ENGINEERING DESIGN PORTFOLIO

Industrial Pipeline
Cleaning & Monitoring Robot

Problem Statement

Conventional pipe maintenance is fraught with danger and inefficiency. Manual inspection cannot accurately detect hairline structural fractures. Our solution addresses the need for a **remote-operated, multi-sensing robotic platform** capable of navigating high-risk environments with zero human exposure.

Electronics & Control

Our control logic relies on a high-speed wireless architecture that allows for sub-100ms latency during navigation.

  • Brain: ESP32 Dual-Core Microcontroller
  • Drive: L298N Dual H-Bridge Motor Driver
  • Streaming: ESP32-CAM for 720p visual monitoring

Prototype Development

Fig 2.0: Multi-Wheel Drive Chassis Assembly

The mechanical structure uses a **central pillar design** with spring-loaded motor modules. This allows the robot to "grip" the internal walls of the pipe, providing enough traction for vertical climbs.

  • ✔ High-Torque 12V DC Gear Motors
  • ✔ Reinforced PVC & 3D Printed Components
  • ✔ Modular wheel tracks for variable diameters
